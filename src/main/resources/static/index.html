Frequently Asked Questions
How do I submit availability?

Go to the Dashboard and fill in the Register Availability form with your preferred date and time window, then click Submit.

How is Total Hours calculated?

Total Hours sums the duration of every shift request you've submitted (end time minus start time) and displays the total in hours and minutes.

How do I contact my manager?

Use the Messages section from the side menu to send messages directly to your manager.
